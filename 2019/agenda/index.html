Agenda

The Legal Innovation and Technology Conference is a single-day single-track conference taking place on Monday, April 8, 2019 from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM at Suffolk Law School, Sargent Hall, 120 Tremont St., Boston, MA 02108. The conference will start with breakfast in the 1st Floor Function Room and end with a reception in the 5th floor Commons and Sky Lounge.
