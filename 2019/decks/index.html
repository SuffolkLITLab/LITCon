LIT Bits

LIT Bits are rapid-fire talks focusing on legal innovation and technology where the speaker is given only 5 min and 20 slides (set to advance automatically every 15 seconds). You can apply to present a LIT Bit here. Below you will find a selection of prior LIT Bits to give you a sense of what we're looking for.

ProTip: You can make a video fill your screen by clicking on the full screen button at the bottom right of the video. This should appear once you start playing the video.
