Agenda

The Legal Innovation and Technology Conference is a single-day single-track conference taking place on Monday, April 3rd, 2023 from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM at Suffolk Law School, Sargent Hall, 120 Tremont St., Boston, MA 02108. The conference will start with breakfast in the 1st Floor Function Room and end with a reception in the 5th floor Commons and Sky Lounge. Presentations will be streamed via a Zoom webinar and archived on our YouTube channel.

2:00 PM - 3:30 PM
Meet Docassemble; Make a Guided Interview

In anticipation of Docacon on the 4th, we'll introduce folks to Docassemble. Docassemble is an open source tool powering much of legal tech. In this session attendees will have the option to work along with our presenter to turn an existing court form into a prototype guided interview. Bring your own internet capable device (wifi will be provided).
